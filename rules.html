6. TRUMP PLAYER REGULATIONS

  • Four Trump players must be declared three days before the tournament start.
  • Trump players cannot be changed once declared.
  • Trump players can only play Trump matches (M4 or M5). They cannot play Regular matches.
  • Regular players may be promoted to Trump matches. However, once a Regular player plays a Trump match, they cannot play Regular matches again.

7. PLAYER LIST SUBMISSION

  • Captains must submit player combinations one day before the match by 6:30 AM.
  • Players must be listed in the exact match order (M1 to M5).
  • If any player is not available to play on match day, the captain must explicitly mention this in writing while submitting the player list. (M1 to M5).

9. INJURY & 10. TIE BREAKS

Player Injury & Replacement:

If a player is injured before or during a match, the opposing captain may select any replacement player from the same team.

Tie Break Rule (Group Stage):

If two teams are tied on points in the group stage, a single 21-point match will be played. Players will be nominated by team captains.

11. FIXTURE SCHEDULING & AUTHORITY

  • The Tournament Committee publishes fixtures. Teams must play as per the scheduled date & time.
  • If a match is postponed, the Committee may reschedule it or schedule other matches instead.
  • All decisions of the Tournament Committee are final and binding.

12. KNOCKOUTS & 13. SCHEDULE

Top 2 teams from each group qualify. Each tie consists of 5 matches.

  • April 15 & 16: Semi-Finals (Grp A Winner vs Grp B Runner-Up, etc.)
  • April 18: 2nd Runner-Up Match
  • April 19: Grand Final

14. INTERPRETATION OF RULES

Any situation not explicitly covered in these rules will be decided by the Tournament Committee, whose decision will be final and binding.
