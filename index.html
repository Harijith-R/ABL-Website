OFFICIAL RULEBOOK

ABL Season 5 Governance & Match Format

1. TIE & MATCH FORMAT

  • 5 Matches per Tie (3 Regular + 2 Trump).
  • 1 Game to 30 Points per match.
  • Interval: 3 minutes at 15 points.
  • Tie-Break: Single 21-point match if teams tie on points.

2. TEAM COMPOSITION

  • Squad Size: Strictly 10 players per team.
  • Mandatory: All 10 players must play one match each.
  • Limit: Maximum 1 match per player per tie.
  • Injuries: Opposing captain selects replacement.
